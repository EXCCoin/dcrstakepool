ExchangeCoin StakePool

General info

A stake pool is used to delegate your voting powers to a third party (the pool). The pool cannot steal your funds. Participating in a stake pool is perfect for people who wish to take part in EXCCoin's proof of stake voting but can't due to:

  • Not wanting to keep a constantly unlocked wallet
  • Lack of a realible/low latency Internet connection

The source code is available at GitHub.
